## Quick Stat Sources
- Industry reports: "X% of [audience] struggle with [problem]"
- Time-based: "Average team spends X hours on [task]"
- Comparison: "X% faster/cheaper/better than [alternative]"
- Social proof: "X developers/companies already using [solution]"
- Problem awareness: "X% don't realize they're [missing opportunity]"

---

## Writing Tips
1. **Stat must shock or relate** - Make them go "wait, really?"
2. **Keep sentences SHORT** - 3-7 words per line
3. **Active voice only** - "BlackRoad deploys" not "is deployed by"
4. **CTA = 1-2 words** - Try it, Start free, Learn more, Join now
5. **Make it feel inevitable** - Like they already decided
