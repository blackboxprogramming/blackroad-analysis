## Writing Rules

1. **One element per line** - keep it scannable
2. **Active voice only** - "We fix" not "is fixed"
3. **"You" and "your"** - make it personal
4. **Concrete over abstract** - "6-month learning curve" not "steep learning curve"
5. **Emotional payoffs use "feels like"** - visceral, not intellectual
6. **Questions create space** - "What if...?" invites imagination
7. **Social proof = direct quotes** - "I deleted 10,000 lines" > "customers love it"
8. **Numbers ground reality** - "$200k/year" > "expensive"
9. **Future pace with "imagine"** - put them in the outcome
10. **CTA = inevitability** - by now, it's obvious
